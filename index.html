Who We Are

At Livio, we are committed to connecting exchange students with like-minded roommates, creating a seamless and stress-free experience for those embarking on their studies abroad.

Our platform simplifies the housing process, offering personalized roommate matches and access to curated housing options near universities worldwide. By blending technology with a human touch, we create communities where students can thrive, collaborate, and feel at home.
